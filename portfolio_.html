Brochure / Flyer / Poster Design

No one can doubt the relevance and effectiveness that brochures, flyers and posters still have in the digital age. For starters, not everyone owns a smart phone or laptop to receive your weekly newsletters in their email addresses, for example. Secondly, most people still appreciate the feel of paper in their hands and reading words written on paper. But most of all people still value something they can fold and put in their pockets after they are done reading, pass on to a friend or crumple up and toss in a waste paper basket :)

Web Scraping

Picture the following scenario. You're online staring at a long table of data (probably with more than a thousand entries) with information that you badly need. Maybe you just need information from one or two columns in each row. The owner of the website has not been kind enough to grace the page with a big download button for your convenience. Copy and paste is out of question because there are high chances that you will pull out unwanted data or nothing at all. What do you do? Copy out the information by hand, one painful row at a time? No way, unless you're into that sort of thing. This is where web scraping comes in...
